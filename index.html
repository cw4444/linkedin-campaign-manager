How the system is meant to work

Clear separation between the planner, the executor, and the approver.

1. Brief agent

Turns the stakeholder ask into a campaign record with budget, goal, audience, and risk flags.

2. Media agent

Suggests targeting, naming, budget splits, and creative rotation, but never publishes directly.

3. MCP stub

Provides simulated audience checks, pacing signals, and optimization drafts for the app to display.

4. Human approver

Reviews the draft, signs off on the change list, and decides what would be safe to execute live.
